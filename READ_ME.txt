# Flappy Bird

Welcome to Flappy Bird, a simple yet addictive web-based game where you control a bird's flight through a series of obstacles.

## How to Play

1. **Start/Resume Button**: Click this button to start or resume the game.
2. **Pause Button**: Click this button to pause the game.
3. **Restart Button**: Click this button to restart the game.
4. **High Score**: Track your highest score as you play.
5. **Speed Game**: Is increased in the intervals of 10 seconds

## Controls

- **Arrow Up**: Move the bird upward.
- **Arrow Left/Right**: Move the bird horizontally.
- **Arrow Down**: Move the bird downward.

## Gameplay

- Guide the bird through openings between pillars by adjusting its position.
- Avoid colliding with the pillars or flying out of bounds.
- The game speeds up periodically, increasing the challenge.
- Your high score is displayed at the top of the screen.
